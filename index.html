About me

Who is behind de codes

My name is Warley Franco (a little different name), I was born in Rio de Janeiro, raised in a small city called Conceição de Macabu, I fell in love by codes on the first time a saw how they work, and after this I never stopped learn, always thinking about quality and best practices.

I've worked in some really nice Brazilian companies like RedZero, Profite, E-Can, Época Cosméticos (Magazine Luiza Group), Bennu S.A. and others, each place I've worked was a great experience and learning who formed my current knowledge and my eye focused to create awesome solutions for business.

How I work?

My development process starts on understanding what kind of challenge we need to dive in.

After this, we need to design how we want it works.

Then we start to create a prototype based on these requirements, just to check if they really solve the challenge

Currently I work with static sites, with responsive features, E-commerces (Vtex, Shopify), Hybrid Mobile Apps, and custom systems

Technologies: ES6, WebPack, React, React Native, Angular, Ionic, NodeJS.

If you need some specific knowledge or technology, please get in touch, I will be glad to learn something new to create a greate solution for you.
